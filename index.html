My Portfolio Some Of My Projects

Delta Robot

As part of a dynamic team, I contributed to the development of a Delta Robot optimized for a critical quality control application. Collaboratively, we implemented ROS as the central control system and integrated a MATLAB-based digital twin to refine the robot's operation. Additionally, we designed and integrated a conveyor system with an advanced camera setup for real-time product imaging. These images were then processed through a collectively trained machine learning model for swift and accurate defect classification. This project exemplifies our team's collective proficiency in robotics and automation, showcasing our ability to deliver high-precision solutions for quality control applications.

Home Surveillance System

Our Home Security System project is designed with one goal in mind: to offer homeowners like you a reliable solution for safeguarding your home against potential thefts, whether you're there or not. We've incorporated a range of powerful features and functionalities to bolster the security of your residence and provide you with the assurance you need for complete peace of mind.

Android App for Charging Station

In my early foray into app development, I created a dynamic charging station solution for startups using Java. The app integrated mapping, real-time availability updates, and booking functionalities. While the initial UI was rudimentary, it provided a robust foundation for future iterations, showcasing the potential for innovative solutions in our local ecosystem

Automatic Stamping Machine

The goal of this project was to develop an Automatic Stamping Machine for ABC Packaging Company. The machine was designed to stamp the company's logo, address, product details, etc., on packeted products, paper/documents, boxes, or bottles. The Automatic Stamping Machine aims to overcome the challenges associated with the current manual process. By automating stamping/printing tasks, the machine increases efficiency, reduces effort, and improves accuracy.
